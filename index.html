The Snakes and Laders


How to play?


  1. Take it in turns to roll dices.
  2. Move your counter forward the number of spaces shown on the dice.
  3. If your counter lands at the bottom of a ladder, you move up to the top of the ladder.
  4. If your counter lands on the head of a snake, you must slide down to the bottom of the snake.
  5. The first player to get to the space that says 'home' is the winner!


* Remember, you have to roll exactly as much you need to step on 'home' field. Otherwise you will bounce from the end of the board.
